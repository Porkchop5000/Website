Creative Work

Here are some links to some of my other creative endeavors. I do some artwork, but for the most part I like to make music. I started with playing the bass. I was in a band and we recorded a record. After that I started to learn guitar and keys. I use Pro Tools for mixing and tracking. For drums I use various samples in Kontact. I record at home using a Focusrite Scarlet 18i8. I just started to learn Reaver because I'm unhappy with Pro Tools. Pro Tools frequently has compatibility issues and while I'm really familiar with the program, I'm tired of spending hours trouble-shooting instead of making music.
