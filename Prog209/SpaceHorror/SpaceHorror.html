Space Horror

You are a smuggler who had a job go very wrong. You are the last remaining of your crew. The rest are captured or dead. You barely escaped capture piloting a stolen escape pod. You've been drifting in space for weeks and the emergency food supplies ran out days ago. Starving, desparate, nearly out of water, and nearly hallucinating, you come across a vessel. Trying to get your head straight you think of a cover story so you can at least get to the nearest colony before someone catches on. Thing is, no one answers when you hail the ship. You figure that if it's abandoned, there should at least be some supplies. You attempt to dock. Being that you were not the pilot in your crew, the landing goes badly damaging your ship. Fortunately you are still mostly intact.
